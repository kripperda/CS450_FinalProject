About the Neighborhood

Prairie Village is a peaceful and well-maintained neighborhood in O'Fallon, Missouri. One of 8 total Villages making up the Villages at Dardenne, the neighborhood offers wonderful amenities for its residents including plenty of walking paths, one of three community pools geared towards families, ponds stocked with various fish and wildlife, a clubhouse available for rent, tennis courts, and plenty of events for families of all ages.
