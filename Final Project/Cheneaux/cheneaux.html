About the Neighborhood

Cheneaux Village is a peaceful and well-maintained neighborhood in Dardenne Prairie, Missouri. One of 8 total Villages making up the Villages at Dardenne, the neighborhood offers wonderful amenities for its residents including over 3 miles of walking paths, a pond stocked with various fish and wildlife, backing to the Links at Dardenne golf course, and plenty of events for families of all ages.
