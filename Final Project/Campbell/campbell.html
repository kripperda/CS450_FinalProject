About the Neighborhood

Campbell Village is a peaceful and well-maintained neighborhood in Dardenne Prairie, Missouri consisting of two-unit villas. One of 8 total Villages making up the Villages at Dardenne, the neighborhood offers wonderful amenities for its residents including over 3 miles of walking paths, one of three community pools geared towards families, ponds stocked with various fish and wildlife, and plenty of events for families of all ages.
