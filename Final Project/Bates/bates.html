About the Neighborhood

Bates Village is a peaceful and well-maintained neighborhood in O'Fallon, Missouri. One of 8 total Villages making up the Villages at Dardenne, the neighborhood offers wonderful amenities for its residents including over a 3 mile nature trail, sidewalks, a pond stocked with various fish and wildlife, a link to The Knolls neighborhood and plenty of events for families of all ages.
