About the Neighborhood

The Villages at Dardenne is a peaceful and well-maintained neighborhood nestled between the two vibrant St. Louis suburban communities of Dardenne Prairie and O'Fallon, Missouri. The neighborhood offers wonderful amenities for its residents including over 10 miles of walking paths, three community pools geared towards families, tennis courts, ponds stocked with various fish and wildlife, and a clubhouse residents can rent for events.

The Villages at Dardenne is a great place to raise a family. Neighbors love the family-friendly vibe and dog-friendly spaces. Children attend schools within the Fort Zumwalt School District with some of the highest success rates in the state in addition to various private schools in the area. Nearby is the Great Rivers Greenway system that links walking and biking paths all throughout the St. Louis area.

In between the surrounding Villages and tucked into Prairie Village is the Links at Dardenne golf course. This 18 hole golf course was crafted ina links-style design, combining the flavor of the courses of yesteryear with all of the modern conveniences of today's game. The course baosts expansive fairways and manicured bentgrass greens surrounded by the sights and sounds of nature.
